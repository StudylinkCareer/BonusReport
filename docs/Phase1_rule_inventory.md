## Doc 1/13: `Chính_sách_chỉ_tiêu__bonus__final_1_6_24.pdf`
**Master Target & Bonus Policy (effective 01/06/2024)** — applies to Counsellor and CO

### Section I — Enrolment Target Policy

**D1.R1 — Definition of 1 enrolment target**
- 1 enrolment target/month = 1 student starts FIRST course this month, where:
  - Course is part of an agreement (thỏa thuận) signed with the Company
  - Course is paid commission (hoa hồng) by the partner
- Second course in same agreement: NOT counted
- New agreement with new course: separate target counted
- Commission ≠ service fee. School paying only service fee (not commission) does NOT count.
- Citation: §I (header) + §I.1 lines

**D1.R2 — Target country list**
- Applies to partners with agreements in: Australia, Canada, USA, New Zealand, UK, Singapore, Ireland, Switzerland, Finland, Netherlands, Germany, France, Malaysia
- Citation: §I (header)

**D1.R3 — KPI scope per role**
- Couns/CO pair KPI: enrolment target + cancellation target
- CO sub-agent KPI: enrolment target only
- Citation: §I.1.1

**D1.R4 — 2-market contract cancellation**
- Customer signs 2-market contract, cancels 1: cancelled market does NOT count toward Couns/CO cancellation target for that market
- Citation: §I.1.2

**D1.R5 — Onshore + summer enrolment date trigger**
- Onshore students: closed enrolment reported on actual enrolment date
- Summer abroad students: reported on date of CoE/LoA issue/Telex note
- Citation: §I.1.3

**D1.R6 — 2-out-target = 1-target rule**
- 2 "out target" cases = 1 target equivalent for that market
- Helps reach/exceed target tier; gets paid at meet/over rate
- The 2 out-target cases themselves: paid at out-target rate (not meet/over)
- US market: 2 out-target = 1.4 target (instead of 1.0)
- Citation: §I.1.4 + §I.2 Lưu ý
- AMBIG: What constitutes "out target" precisely? Likely refers to out-of-system or master agent referral cases. Need to define against case classification rules.

**D1.R7 — Enrolment-only file routing**
- Enrolment-only file (school pays full commission) WITH counselling needed: handled by Couns+CO pair, both get bonus
- Enrolment-only file WITHOUT counselling: handled by CO solo, CO gets bonus only
- Citation: §I.1.5

**D1.R8 — No-visa or no-enrolment-with-fee handling**
- Cases without visa granted OR without enrolment, but service fee was collected
- Pay rate: "Out-system / Fees paid yet visa refused / Extra high risk incl. clients undergoing migration application"
- EXCEPTION: Service fee that is deposit-only (e.g., AU Standard Plus 3M deposit) → NO bonus
- Citation: §I.1.6

**D1.R9 — VN-domestic (du học tại chỗ) staffing rule**
- "Du học tại chỗ" (study abroad domestically in VN) cases: handled by Counsellor solo
- CO does NOT participate
- Citation: §I.1.7

### Section I.3 — Bonus payment rules per role

**D1.R11 — Couns/CO pair payment rules**
- Sub-case A (Enrolled before visa granted, i.e., enrolment_date < visa_date):
  - Counsellor: 100% bonus
  - CO: 50% bonus
  - When visa later issued + CO closes file: CO receives remaining 50%
- Sub-case B (Visa granted before enrolment, i.e., enrolment_date > visa_date) AND CO has closed file:
  - Counsellor: 100% bonus
  - CO: 100% bonus
- Citation: §I.3.1

**D1.R12 — CO sub-agent payment rules**
- Applies to CO doing only enrolment (ghi danh) for sub-agent, OR CO doing full-service for sub-agent
- Sub-case A (Enrolled before visa granted):
  - CO: 50% bonus
  - When visa later granted + file closed: CO receives remaining 50%
- Sub-case B (Visa granted before enrolment) AND CO has closed file:
  - CO: 100% bonus
- Citation: §I.3.2

### Section I.4 — New employee bonuses

**D1.R13 — New Counsellor probation: CO duty for first file per market**
- New Counsellor (no experience): performs CO role for FIRST file in EACH market they handle, for training purposes
- During training: bonus only counted for files they handled as Counsellor (not CO)
- Citation: §I.4.1

**D1.R14 — Probation completion bonus**
- New Counsellor/CO who passes probation + signs employment contract: receives bonus on probation-period files when:
  - Student gets visa granted + enrolled, OR
  - Visa refused/cancelled but Company collected service fee
- Citation: §I.4.2

**D1.R15 — New Counsellor handover (no target counting)**
- New Counsellor receiving handover from departing Counsellor: handover clients do NOT count toward new Counsellor's target
- EXCEPTION: If client's study plan totally changes (different from departing Counsellor's plan) and is reassigned to new Counsellor for re-counselling → new Counsellor DOES get target credit; old Counsellor does NOT get target/bonus
- Citation: §I.4.3

### Section I.5 — Bonus deductions

**D1.R16 — Customer/partner serious complaint deduction**
- Serious complaint due to staff negligence: full bonus deducted EITHER for the month of complaint OR up to date of complaint
- Triggers include: customer cancellation due to complaint, customer escalation to BGĐ (Board of Directors), social media bashing, partner termination requests
- Citation: §I.5.1

**D1.R17 — Post-departure complaint**
- If complaint arises during employee handover OR after departure: ALL post-departure bonus is forfeited
- Citation: §I.5.2

**D1.R18 — Commission claw-back**
- If bonus paid but student doesn't enrol / school doesn't pay commission: difference (including team/pair excess bonus) deducted from future bonuses
- If no future bonus available: employee must transfer back (even after departure)
- Citation: §I.5.3

**D1.R19 — CO refusing handover (Senior CO)**
- CO refusing handover instructed by superior (when total Current files including handover ≤40 for CO, ≤60 for Senior CO): full bonus deducted up to date of refusal
- Note: CO cannot refuse handover when ordered by superior under these volume caps
- Citation: §I.5.4

### Section I.6 — Departure bonus payment

**D1.R20 — Departure: pre-lodge, no enrolment**
- Couns/CO leaves before visa lodged AND student not enrolled: NO bonus
- Two options for new CO:
  - **Option A**: New CO accepts target handover from departing CO → 100% bonus + target counts when student enrols + visa + file close
  - **Option B**: New CO does NOT accept target handover but processes handed-over file → bonus at "Under Target" rate, no target credit, plus monthly allowance:
    - 500,000 VND/month × 6 months for 1-5 files (pre-lodge, pre-enrolment)
    - 1,000,000 VND/month × 6 months for 6-10 files
    - 1,500,000 VND/month × 6 months for 11-15 files
- Note: CO required to accept handover from CO when ordered by superior
- Citation: §I.6.1 + page 4 elaboration

**D1.R21 — Departure: post-lodge or post-visa, no enrolment**
- Couns/CO leaves after visa lodged (waiting result) OR has visa granted but not enrolled
- Departing Couns/CO: 50% bonus at Under Target rate when student enrols + new CO closes file
- New CO: 50% bonus at Under Target rate, NO target credit on handover file
- Citation: §I.6.2

**D1.R22 — Departure: post-enrolment, no visa**
- Couns/CO leaves when student enrolled but visa not yet granted
- Departing Counsellor: 100% bonus
- Departing CO: 50% bonus
- New CO (taking handover for visa lodgment + file close): 50% bonus at Under Target rate, NO target credit
- Citation: §I.6.3

**D1.R23 — 6-month settlement period**
- After Couns/CO departure: bonus payment begins 6 months after official departure date
- Reason: time for cancellations/refunds/commission settlements
- Citation: §I.6.4

**D1.R24 — Post-departure data theft penalty**
- Evidence of post-departure customer data theft (Confidentiality breach): ALL bonus forfeited
- Citation: §I.6.5

### Section I.7 — Team/pair excess bonuses

**D1.R25 — National Couns/CO team excess (excludes sub-agent)**
- Whole-country Counsellor/CO team (all markets) exceeds monthly enrolment target:
  - 10M VND quick bonus on team report
  - 10M VND additional after Finance confirms 100% enrolment
  - Distributed by file contribution
  - 20% retained for team activities fund
- Citation: §I.7.1

**D1.R26 — Pair excess bonus (≥3/month target)**
- Couns/CO pair with target ≥3 enrolments/month, exceeds monthly target (all markets):
  - 2M VND quick bonus on team report
  - 1M VND additional after Finance confirms 100% enrolment
- Citation: §I.7.2

**D1.R27 — Sub-agent team excess**
- Sub-agent team exceeds monthly enrolment target:
  - 3M VND quick bonus on team report
  - 3M VND additional after Finance confirms 100% enrolment
- Citation: §I.7.3

### Section II — Contract Target Bonus Policy

**D1.R28 — Contract target scope**
- Contract target = additional KPI for COUNSELLOR position only (alongside enrolment KPI)
- Does NOT apply to CO position (in general — but see exception D1.R29)
- Citation: §II.1

**D1.R29 — Contract bonus payment timing**
- Contract bonus paid per service package the Counsellor signs with each customer
- Paid early next month, after QM and Finance complete reports
- CO MAY receive contract bonus depending on the package signed by Counsellor
- Citation: §II.2

**D1.R30 — Counsellor/Sales team contract excess**
- Whole Counsellor/Sales team (excludes sub-agent) exceeds monthly contract target (all markets):
  - 5M VND quick bonus
  - Distributed by file contribution
  - 20% retained for team fund
- Citation: §II.3

**D1.R31 — HCM/ĐN/HN Counsellor monthly contract excess**
- HCM/ĐN/HN Counsellors exceeding monthly contract target (all markets):
  - **i**: Per excess: +100,000 VND (when 2 ≤ target < 4) OR +200,000 VND (when target ≥ 4) for ALL contracts in the month
    - Example 1: target 2-4, 1 over → +1.1M for whole month's contracts
    - Example 2: target ≥4, 1 over → +1.2M for whole month's contracts
  - **ii**: 3 consecutive months exceeding contract target → +200,000 VND per contract for total quarter contracts
  - **iii**: Paid after total contract report at end of month/quarter
- Citation: §II.4 main

**D1.R32 — Premium tier (HCM/ĐN/HN Counsellors)**
- Triggered when:
  - Target ≥ 4 AND
  - Counsellor achieves >10 contracts/month, OR doubled the original target
- Premium rate: 2,200,000 VND per contract for excess contracts above 10 OR above doubled target
  - Example 1: Target 8/month, actual 12 → contracts 1-10 at standard excess rate, contracts 11-12 at premium 2.2M each
  - Example 2: Target 4/month, actual 9 → contracts 1-8 at standard excess, contract 9 at premium 2.2M
- Premium paid AFTER student enrolment
- Citation: §II.4.b

**D1.R33 — Conditions for contract counting (asterisk *) — apply to D1.R31 and D1.R32**
- Counsellor's contract target counted for the month when:
  1. Customer signs agreement IN the month
  2. Pays service fee IN the month
  3. In-system contracts ≥ 80% of total contracts
  4. Quality contracts: visa pass rate >75% per internal evaluation
- Direct contracts signed enrolling via master agent/agent: counted as 0.5
- Citation: §II.4 (*) bottom

**D1.R34 — Eligible contract packages for excess bonus**
- Eligible packages and markets:
  - 6M, 9M, 20M VND (difficult cases, high-risk regions): AUS, NZ, UK, SGP
  - 9.5M, 14M, 15M (Master) VND: Canada
  - **Special**: 7.5M VND Canada: excess = 80,000 VND (2≤target<4) or 140,000 VND (target≥4)
  - 16M, 45M VND: USA
- Other packages: standard rate per spec, excess via mechanism above with rates determined by BGĐ per case
- Total monthly contracts = total contracts Counsellor closed in month at standard rate per market/package
- Citation: §II.4 (*) packages section
